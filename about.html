About Aetherion Capital

Overview

Aetherion Capital is a private investment corporation specializing in next-generation AI-driven financial technologies. Founded in 2022, we operate as a vetted institutional entity focused on market transparency, predictive analytics, and autonomous trading systems. Our mission is to provide secure, compliant, and high-performance investment solutions exclusively to elite partners, corporations, and select high-net-worth individuals.

Founders & Leadership

Aetherion Capital was founded by a group of former executives from leading tech and finance firms, including alumni from BlackRock, JPMorgan, and Silicon Valley AI labs. The current leadership includes:

  • Dr. Elena Voss — CEO & Chief AI Architect (ex-Google DeepMind, 15+ years in machine learning)
  • Marcus Hale — Chief Investment Officer (ex-Goldman Sachs, 20+ years in quantitative finance)
  • James K. Rourke — Head of Compliance & Regulatory Affairs (former SEC advisor)

Project Horizon Funding & Development

Project Horizon, our flagship AI platform, was funded through a private consortium of institutional investors and early community contributors. Total funding raised: **$420 million** across three rounds (2022–2024).

  • Seed Round (2022): $80 million — led by private equity firms and tech VCs
  • Series A (2023): $150 million — included contributions from vetted community members
  • Series B (2024): $190 million — final round with institutional partners

The community played a vital role: Early vetted supporters provided $45 million in private funding, earning permanent **Legacy Access** privileges in return.

Taxation & Compliance Structure

Aetherion Capital operates under strict regulatory oversight in multiple jurisdictions (US, Cayman Islands, Singapore). All transactions incur small, automated fees:

  • **Trade Fee**: 0.08%–0.15% per executed trade (covers execution & compliance)
  • **Withdrawal Fee**: 0.05%–0.1% per withdrawal (covers tax clearing & regulatory reporting)

These fees are automatically deducted at the time of transaction, ensuring full tax compliance and seamless reporting to relevant authorities. This structure maintains economic stability while delivering high-performance results to members.
